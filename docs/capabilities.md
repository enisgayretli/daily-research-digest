# Capabilities

Daily Research Digest helps users stay informed about the most important research developments from the last 24 hours.

The skill continuously focuses on discovering, filtering, and summarizing high-signal research activity across academic publications, models, datasets, benchmarks, open-source projects, and research announcements.

---

## Daily Research Monitoring

Track important developments published during the last 24 hours.

Capabilities:

* New papers
* New models
* New datasets
* New benchmarks
* New repositories
* Research announcements

---

## Research Briefings

Generate concise daily briefings designed for researchers, engineers, founders, analysts, and technical teams.

Capabilities:

* Team briefings

## Signal Prioritization

Daily Research Digest prioritizes:

1. Novelty
2. Scientific significance
3. Practical impact
4. Community attention
5. Benchmark evidence
6. Code availability
7. Open-source adoption
8. Topic relevance

---

* Daily Research Digest
* AI Research Digest

* Open Source Research Briefing
* Executive Research Briefing
* Team Research Briefing
* Topic-Specific Daily Digest

---

## Core Principle

The goal is not to collect the most information.

The goal is to identify the most important developments from today and explain why they matter.
