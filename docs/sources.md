# Sources

Daily Research Digest monitors multiple research, open-source, and industry sources to identify the most important developments from the last 24 hours.

The goal is not to maximize volume.

The goal is to maximize signal.

## Ranking Factors

Daily Research Digest prioritizes:

1. Novelty
2. Scientific significance
3. Practical impact
4. Community attention
5. Code availability
6. Benchmark evidence
7. Open-source adoption
8. Topic relevance

---

## Source Selection Strategy

Search broadly.

Filter aggressively.

Remove duplicates.

Prefer metadata over raw content.

Prefer high-signal developments.

Focus on the most important research activity from the last 24 hours.
