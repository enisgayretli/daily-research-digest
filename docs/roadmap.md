# Roadmap

* Daily research digests
* New paper discovery
* Model monitoring
* Dataset monitoring
* Benchmark monitoring

* Multi-source aggregation

## Long-Term Vision

Daily Research Digest aims to become a research intelligence layer that helps users:

* Understand what happened today
* Detect important research signals early
* Follow emerging scientific trends
* Monitor models, datasets, benchmarks, and repositories
* Reduce information overload
* Stay informed without spending hours searching

The goal is not to collect the most information.

The goal is to identify the most important developments and explain why they matter.
