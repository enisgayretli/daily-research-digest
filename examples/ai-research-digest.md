# AI Research Digest

## Goal

Generate a daily research digest covering the most important AI developments from the last 24 hours.

## Example Prompt

What happened today in artificial intelligence research?

## Focus Areas

* New papers
* New models
* New benchmarks
* New datasets
* Open-source projects

## Expected Output

* Executive Summary
* Most Important Papers
* New Models
* New Datasets
* Key Trends
* Recommended Reading
